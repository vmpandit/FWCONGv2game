## ğŸ® Gameplay Guide

### Starting a Training Session

1. **Launch the game** - Open `index.html` in a browser
2. **Click "New Game"** - Opens agent registration
3. **Enter your name** - Used in scenarios and reports
4. **Select your role** - Choose the track matching your job function
5. **Begin training** - Face role-appropriate scenarios

### During Training

- **Read scenarios carefully** - Examine artifacts for red flags
- **Select answers A-D** - Use keyboard or click
- **Review explanations** - Learn from every decision
- **Track your stats** - Monitor security score, XP, reputation
- **Defeat the boss** - Complete each chapter

### After Training

- **View your report** - Comprehensive performance analysis
- **Print for records** - Compliance documentation
- **Identify weak areas** - Focus future training
- **Retrain as needed** - Try again to improve
